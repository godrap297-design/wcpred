Fair Play Rules

Prediction Rules

One Entry

Each participant may submit only once. Duplicate phone numbers are blocked automatically.

Deadline

Entries must be submitted before the countdown reaches zero or before the admin locks submissions.

Valid Bracket

Every winner must come from the previous match path, so impossible predictions cannot be submitted.

Scoring

Points are awarded when your predicted winner matches the actual team advancing in that round.

Ties

If scores are tied, earlier submission time ranks higher. LCK Dreams may announce additional tie-breakers if needed.

Admin Decisions

The organizing team maintains the official team list, actual winners, scoring configuration, and final results.
